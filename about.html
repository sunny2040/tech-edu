MICRO-TECH INSTITUTE OF EDUCATION

"Microtech - Institute of Education is called “microtech”. Micro-tech is the valuable name of MICRO-TEACH. The mission started in 2023 for youth employment, and this mission has about 100 associated learning centers countrywide, with this number increasing every day. Microtech Institute is the biggest educational institution providing a great platform in the IT sector in the Indian subcontinent. It is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company with a mission to promote IT education and awareness to build a strong foundation for future careers. It is dedicated to fulfilling the basic needs in computer education."

"It is a matter of great pride and pleasure that our country is rapidly developing in economy and education. Given India's large population, enhancing human capital is crucial for national development. According to a study by the World Bank, human capital development is more critical than physical capital for India. Ensuring access to at least 25% of the relevant age group (between 17 to 23 years) for higher education is necessary to meet the country's developmental needs. Microtech Institute of Education is an autonomous organization registered under the Companies Act, 2013 by the Government of Bihar, Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India. In this context, Micro-Tech Institute of Education aims to provide IT education across various grades and courses to the people of the Indian subcontinent. Our organization is committed and dedicated to achieving its goals by focusing on providing accessible and quality education, preparing students directly for further studies and careers in IT and business economics."
